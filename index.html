How to book the wheels.

For 8 people, the clean move is a 13-seater minibus hourly charter. It keeps the group together and makes the countryside/ferry-terminal hops painless.

Booking target

Search for “13-seater minibus Singapore hourly charter” or “private minibus Singapore day charter”. Published guides commonly show hourly charters around S$75/hour with a 3–4 hour minimum; full-day quotes vary by operator and stops.

13-seater / Toyota Hiace style vehicle
8 passengers, light day bags
6–8 hour charter window
Multiple stops included
